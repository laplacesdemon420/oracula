VOTING SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

Contract initiates a data_request (that comes with a question and a resolution source)
DVM voters respond to these requests
Token holders are incentivized to vote correctly

Voter Commit Phase
- token holder commit a hash of the data they believe is correct

Voter Reveal and Reward Phase
At the end of the reveal period, the oracle calculates the verified price and pays
rewards to voters that vote correctly. Other voters are penalized.

Rewards are paid to incentivize i) voter participation and ii) accurate voting.
